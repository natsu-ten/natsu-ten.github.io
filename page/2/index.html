centos7-关闭ipv6
1.在/etc/sysctl.conf中添加此规则：net.ipv6.conf.all.disable_ipv6 = 1 2.在/etc/sysconfig/network中添加此规则：NETWORKING_IPV6 = no 3.在/etc/sysconfig/network-scripts/ifcfg-ethX中添加此规则：IPV6INIT = no 4.关闭防火墙的开机自启动服务：systemctl disable ip6tables.service 5.重新加载sysctl配置或者重启网络#sysctl -por#systemctl restart network
